Facing ill-wishers

We all face ill-wishers. Some more often, some less often...

Show more

Dear haters, teachers and other evilspirits, thank you, oddly enough, for your contribution. When the situation escalates, we have to make an emergency choice. And then we begin to understand better who we are and what we are meant for. And we begin to act more actively and purposefully. So if it wasn't for you, maybe we wouldn't be here.

Irritate!

There will always be dissatisfied with the noise you make, dissatisfied... it doesn't matter with what... dissatisfied with anything related to your activities. So... irritate! ...or don't annoy... it's up to you , but sometimes you just have to sacrifice something for the sake something. And what to sacrifice is up to you too
